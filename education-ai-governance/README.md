---

## Focus Areas

- Student data privacy (FERPA / COPPA)
- Algorithmic bias in grading, assessment, and placement
- Transparency and explainability in AI-supported decisions
- Human-in-the-loop oversight for high-stakes outcomes

---

## In-Scope AI Use Cases

- AI-assisted grading and feedback systems
- Student risk and intervention prediction models
- Admissions and placement decision support
- Personalized learning and recommendation systems

---

## Governance Priorities

- Clear accountability for AI-driven recommendations
- Bias testing and disparate impact analysis
- Student and parent transparency
- Appeal and override mechanisms for AI-supported decisions
