# Enterprise 90-Day AI Governance Playbook

## Executive Implementation Guide
**Kwame Thomas | Responsible AI & Governance**

---

## Purpose

This playbook outlines a practical, repeatable approach for establishing
Responsible AI governance across regulated and public-interest domains.

It is designed for use by:
- AI Governance leaders
- Risk and Compliance teams
- Public-sector and regulated enterprises

The framework is **sector-agnostic**, with controls tailored as needed
for education, real estate, and regulated retail environments.

---

## Days 1–30: Discover & Assess

### Objectives
- Establish visibility into AI usage
- Assign accountability
- Identify immediate risk exposure

### Key Actions
- Inventory all AI systems and AI-assisted decision processes
- Classify systems by impact level (low, medium, high)
- Assign executive owners and operational stewards
- Conduct initial bias, privacy, and compliance risk assessments
- Identify regulatory obligations by sector

### Deliverables
- AI system inventory
- Initial AI risk register
- Accountability and ownership map

---

## Days 31–60: Design & Control

### Objectives
- Implement governance guardrails
- Reduce high-priority risks
- Standardize decision oversight

### Key Actions
- Define human-in-the-loop requirements for high-impact decisions
- Establish explainability and transparency standards
- Implement bias mitigation and testing protocols
- Formalize vendor and third-party AI risk requirements
- Draft and approve AI governance policies

### Deliverables
- Governance control framework
- Human accountability model
- Bias and transparency standards
- Vendor AI risk criteria

---

## Days 61–90: Operate & Monitor

### Objectives
- Operationalize governance
- Enable ongoing oversight
- Prepare for audits and regulatory review

### Key Actions
- Deploy monitoring, logging, and audit mechanisms
- Establish appeal, escalation, and override processes
- Produce executive AI risk dashboards
- Conduct governance tabletop or simulation exercise
- Prepare audit-ready documentation

### Deliverables
- Monitoring and audit artifacts
- Executive reporting package
- Documented escalation and appeal workflows

---

## Outcomes at Day 90

By the end of 90 days, the organization can confidently state:
- We know where AI is used
- We understand our highest AI risks
- We have accountability for AI outcomes
- We can explain, challenge, and correct AI decisions
- We are prepared for regulatory and public scrutiny

---

## Strategic Value

This playbook enables organizations to:
- Scale AI responsibly
- Reduce legal and reputational risk
- Build public trust
- Align innovation with governance

It transforms Responsible AI from principle into **operational reality**.
